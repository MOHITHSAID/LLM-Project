Location: Mysuru, Karnataka, India

History:
Residence of the Wadiyar royal family. Rebuilt in 1912 after fire damage.

Architecture:
Indo-Saracenic style combining Hindu, Muslim, Rajput, and Gothic elements.

Cultural Significance:
Major attraction during Dussehra festival.

Tourism Information:
Illuminated with thousands of lights during festivals.

Interesting Facts:
One of the most visited palaces in India.
