Location: Hyderabad, Telangana, India

History:
Built in 1591 by Muhammad Quli Qutb Shah to mark the founding of Hyderabad.

Architecture:
Square structure with four grand minarets and arches. Indo-Islamic architectural style.

Cultural Significance:
Symbol of Hyderabad city and major cultural landmark.

Tourism Information:
Nearby markets like Laad Bazaar attract tourists.

Interesting Facts:
The monument was built near a historic trade route.
