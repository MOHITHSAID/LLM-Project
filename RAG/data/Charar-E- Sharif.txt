Location: Jammu and Kashmir, India

History:
Charar-e-Sharif is a revered Muslim shrine dedicated to Sheikh Noor-ud-Din Noorani, a respected Kashmiri saint from the 15th century.

Architecture:
Traditional wooden Kashmiri architecture with sloping roofs and intricate carvings.

Cultural Significance:
Major pilgrimage site for Muslims in Kashmir and symbol of regional spiritual heritage.

Tourism Information:
Visited by devotees and tourists seeking spiritual experience.

Interesting Facts:
The shrine has been rebuilt multiple times due to natural and historical events.
