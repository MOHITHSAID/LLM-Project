Location: Amritsar, Punjab, India

History:
Founded by Guru Ram Das and completed by Guru Arjan Dev in the 16th century.

Architecture:
Gold-covered shrine surrounded by a sacred water tank called Amrit Sarovar.

Cultural Significance:
Most important pilgrimage site for Sikhs.

Tourism Information:
Langar (free community kitchen) serves thousands daily.

Interesting Facts:
Visitors from all religions are welcome.
